Everything you need

A complete platform,
without the platform

Every tool your team needs — messaging, task management, documents, whiteboarding, file sharing, and calls — running peer-to-peer with no servers between you.

Chat

Encrypted messaging with message editing, emoji reactions, @mentions, markdown rendering, typing indicators, and presence status. Real conversations, fully private.

Board

Kanban-style task boards synced across your team in real time. Drag cards between columns, assign work, and track progress — all conflict-free with CRDTs.

Documents

Collaborative documents with real-time CRDT-based editing powered by Yrs. Multiple people editing simultaneously with automatic conflict resolution. No merge conflicts, ever.

Canvas

Shared whiteboard for visual collaboration. Sketch diagrams, map ideas, and brainstorm together — synced across all participants in real time.

Drive

Peer-to-peer file sharing and storage. Transfer files directly between nodes — no cloud middleman, no upload limits, no storage fees.

Calls

Voice and video calls routed peer-to-peer over the encrypted mesh. No call servers, no recording by third parties, no quality compromises.

Privacy by architecture

Not just encrypted.
Structurally private.

There are no servers to subpoena. No cloud to breach. No company holding your data. Communitas is peer-to-peer — your data lives on your devices and nowhere else.

Post-quantum encryption

ML-DSA-87 signatures for user identity (Level 5, 192-bit quantum security). Built to withstand both classical and quantum attacks.

Partition-tolerant

Works when the internet doesn't. Your team keeps collaborating across network partitions — CRDTs reconcile state automatically when connections restore.

No servers. Ever.

Every node is a first-class citizen. No central servers, no cloud accounts, no infrastructure to manage or trust. The network is its participants.

Trust you control

Contacts are categorised: blocked, unknown, known, trusted. You decide who can reach you and what access they get.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No accounts. No sign-ups. No cloud configuration. Install, connect, collaborate.

1

Install the network

Install the x0x daemon — the peer-to-peer gossip layer that Communitas runs on. One command, 30 seconds.

2

Launch Communitas

Open the app. Your cryptographic identity is generated automatically. No email, no password, no phone number required.

3

Start collaborating

Add contacts by their identity, form groups, and use the full platform. Everything is encrypted end-to-end from the first message.
